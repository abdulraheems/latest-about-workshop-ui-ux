About Innoovatum Engineering & Technology LLP

Innoovatum Engineering & Technology LLP is a distinguished Software & Technology Company headquartered in Bangalore & Vijayapura, India, with 10 years of impactful presence. Established with a vision of making engineering education a practical approach with the motto "Achieve Great Things," Innoovatum is recognized by Startup India and incubated under the Government of Karnataka startup initiative. .
